PdOM Context: Mission Impact Assessment

This view shows how SDUs are organized and what phase of the PdOM process they're currently in. Teams in the Mission Impact Assessment phase are focused on:

  • Identifying key customers and stakeholders
  • Documenting workflow inefficiencies and capability gaps
  • Validating that requirements will lead to measurable operational gains
