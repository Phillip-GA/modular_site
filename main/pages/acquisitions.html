About the Acquisition Process

The acquisition process ensures that all technology purchases align with organizational needs and undergo proper evaluation. This process helps identify the best solutions while ensuring compliance with procurement policies and budget constraints.

The process typically takes 8-12 weeks to complete, so plan accordingly in your project timeline. Early engagement with the acquisition team is highly recommended to streamline the process.
